Q&A

Question: How do I enter an event?

Answer: Toggle to the sign up page and your full name, select an event and your grade level

Question: Where do I see the reports?
Answer: Toggle to the reports page, you will view the names and points of each student, organized by grade level.
A notification will appear at the end of each quarter to alert you of this
Question: How do I see the see the winners?
Answer: Toggle to the rewards page. Winners with the most points in each grade level will be displayed along with the prize they win
Choose a grade level to generate a random winner and see the prize that they win
A notification will appear at the end of each quarter to alert you of this
Question: How do prizes work?
Answer: Each event you participate in will earn you a point, to win the school spirit item you must have at least 1 point.
You must earn at least 4 points to win the food prize and at least 7 to win the Gold Medal
